Kigali Genocide Memorial

A powerful historical site honoring the victims of the 1994 genocide. It offers education, reflection, and remembrance.

Mount Kigali

A popular hiking destination offering panoramic views of Kigali city, perfect for adventure lovers and photographers.

Inema Arts Center

A vibrant art gallery showcasing contemporary Rwandan art, music performances, and creative workshops.
